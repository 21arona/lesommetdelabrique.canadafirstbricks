RECOGNITION OF EXHIBITORS' WORK

We understand that a successful exhibition relies on exhibitors of MOCs. Therefore, starting this year, we aim to recognize the contribution of some of you by distinguishing exhibitors based on the models exhibited. We recognize the following types of exhibitors:

🔹 Standard Exhibitors: Someone who exhibits a unique collection of LEGO® models.

🔹 Premium Exhibitors: Someone who exhibits one or more MOCs.

🔹 Platinum Exhibitors: Someone who exhibits one or more large-scale/complex MOCs.

🔹 Prestige Exhibitors: Exhibitor who has won an award from the previous edition.

These distinctions will be made during the exhibition by a jury and will grant, for premium, platinum, or prestige exhibitors, certain benefits for the next year's event.

Awards for Exhibitors

Here is a list of the awards that will be presented at the 2025 edition of Le Sommet de la Brique. Please note that only one prize can be won per participant.

🔹 Rookie of the Year
Award presented by the jury to the exhibitor who is attending their first exhibition and has stood out the most with their MOC. This recognition comes with a $100 coupon for Canada First Bricks store.

🔹 Young Exhibitor of the Year
Award presented by the jury to the exhibitor under 18 who has stood out the most with their MOC. This recognition comes with a LEGO® set provided by Canada First Bricks.

🔹 Best Use of the Theme
Award presented by the jury to the exhibitor who best used the theme in their MOC. This recognition comes with a $100 coupon for Canada First Bricks store.

🔹 Best Use of LEDs
Award presented by the jury to the exhibitor who best used LEDs in their MOC. This recognition comes with a $100 coupon for Canada First Bricks store.

🔹 1st Place Visitor Vote
Award presented to the exhibitor who received the most votes from visitors. This recognition comes with a $200 coupon for Canada First Bricks store.

🔹 1st Place Jury Vote
Award presented to the exhibitor who received the most votes from the jury. This recognition comes with a $300 coupon for Canada First Bricks store.

🔹 1st Place Exhibitor Vote
Award presented to the exhibitor who received the most votes from other exhibitors/congress participants. This recognition comes with a $500 coupon for Canada First Bricks store.
