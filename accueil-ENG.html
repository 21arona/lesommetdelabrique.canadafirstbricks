Le Sommet de la Brique

is a convention/exhibition for Lego® enthusiasts Lego® who gather to share their passion and display their own creations MOC (My Own Creation) or their unique collection of Lego® models.

CONVENTION EXHIBITION

Présenté par

This annual event held at Espace St-Hyacinthe, with an exhibition taking place the first weekend of October, will always feature its convention in the days preceding the exhibition. For the 2025 edition, the convention dates are October 2-3 and the exhibition will take place on October 4-5, with a VIP event on the evening of October 3.
