EXHIBITOR INFO

Hello fellow LEGO Enthusiast! Are you looking for regular single day tickets?

Looking for more from your LEGO fan experience? Want to really get involved with the community? Well then the full 4 day convention is for you!

Bricks in the Six, Toronto’s largest LEGO fan event, is returning November 14-17, 2024! The 4 day convention is designed for adult fans of LEGO who truly love everything to do with those tiny little bricks. It will feature workshops, games, activities, and exclusive interactive experiences, as well as offer you an opportunity to display your models for the public.

All ages are welcome at the 4 day convention, and children under 16 must be supervised by an adult at all times. Activities for the 4 day convention will be primarily geared towards an adult audience.

To register as an Exhibitor, you’ll need to log in with an account first. If you registered as an Exhibitor in 2023, you can use the same login info as last year. Once logged in, head to the Shop and grab your Exhibitior registration there, or go to the Hotel Booking page to find the link to book your room for the event.

The cost to register is $100, and all exhibitors and attendees are expected to adhere to our Code of Conduct.

If you don’t have an account, you can create one here.

A full schedule of events will be posted closer to the date – convention attendess can expect that most of the bigger panels and workshops will be held during the day on Thursday and Friday, with a Thursday night mixer event at the hotel, and additional events on Friday and Saturday evening as well.



This year’s event will be at the Hilton Meadowvale/Mississauga Hotel, conveniently located along Highway 401. There will be free parking for overnight guests at the hotel, and a room rate of $159/night for the event (plus taxes and fees).

If you have any questions, reach out to our team at info@brickassembly.ca (We are still updating some information on our website, so bear with us as we do so!)
